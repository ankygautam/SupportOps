# SupportOps Demo Guide

## Demo accounts

| Role | Email | Password | Best used for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Admin | `admin@supportops.dev` | `supportops` | Full platform walkthrough, analytics, team management, settings, launch checklist |
| Team Lead | `lead@supportops.dev` | `supportops` | Queue health, ticket escalation, incidents, SLA monitoring |
| Support Agent | `agent1@supportops.dev` | `supportops` | Ticket handling, customer context, comments, internal notes |

## Recommended walkthrough

1. Start at `/demo` for guided role selection.
2. Sign in as `Team Lead` for the strongest operations story, or `Admin` for the broadest product tour.
3. Open Dashboard to frame queue pressure, incidents, and SLA exposure.
4. Open Tickets and switch to a saved view like `Critical Queue` or `Waiting on Customer`.
5. Open a ticket detail view to show comments, internal notes, escalation, linked incident context, and workflow actions.
6. Open Incidents to show affected services and linked tickets.
7. Open SLA Monitor to explain urgency handling.
8. Open Analytics and Team for leadership/admin depth.
9. Open Settings for persisted preferences and role-aware controls.
10. Use `/launch-checklist` at the end to reinforce readiness and packaging quality.

## Best role by audience

- Product/design audience: `Admin`
- Engineering audience: `Team Lead`
- Workflow/support audience: `Support Agent`
